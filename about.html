Hello and Xin chào, I am Huy, and I am from Hanoi, Vietnam. Currently, I am an Erasmus Mundus master student studying the Master's program in Astrophysics and Space Science (MASS).


The questions that keep me up at night are: How did the Universe begin? What is the nature of dark matter and dark energy? How do the grand structures form from the primordial fluctuations? What can we learn about our Universe through galaxies distribution? I am passionate about exploring these questions through both simulations and observation.


You can find more about my academic journey in my CV, my current and past research in Research. I also love to travel and explore new places, which you can see in my Travel.
